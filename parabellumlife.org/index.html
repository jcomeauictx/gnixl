Parabellum Life

Si vis pacem, para bellum.

The Parabellum life is one of constant readiness for battle. It is for those who have seen the historical tendency of strong men creating a better world for their children, only to watch their children and grandchildren destroy it. We reject the idea that there should be generations of poets, painters, and philosophers with no grounding in the art of defense. Every man should be as comfortable with his rifle as he is with his books.

If you resolve to prepare yourself likewise: you are Parabellum. Welcome.
